Policies & Terms

Clear policies protect both our clients and our driver. By booking with EliteRide LLC, you agree to the following key terms and professional boundaries.

Bookings & Confirmations

  • All trips must be booked and confirmed in writing (email or text).
  • A trip is considered confirmed once fare and details are agreed and payment is received or terms are agreed in writing.
  • EliteRide LLC reserves the right to decline any trip that is unsafe, unlawful, or outside of our reasonable service area.

Cancellation & No-Show Policy

  • Cancellations made 24 hours or more before the scheduled pickup time: no cancellation fee (full refund of prepaid amounts).
  • Cancellations made within 24 hours of pickup may be charged 50% of the quoted fare.
  • Cancellations made within 2 hours of pickup or no-shows at pickup location may be charged 100% of the quoted fare.
  • No-show is defined as the passenger not arriving within 30 minutes of scheduled pickup time and not responding to phone/text.

Wait Time & Extra Stops

  • Reasonable wait time at pickup is included (typically up to 15 minutes for local trips and up to 45 minutes for airport arrivals after actual landing time).
  • Additional wait time may be billed at an hourly or per-15-minute rate, disclosed in advance when possible.
  • Extra stops or route changes not agreed in advance may result in adjusted pricing.
